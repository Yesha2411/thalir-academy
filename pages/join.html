Start Your Learning Journey

Thalir Academy welcomes rural youth, women, and aspiring learners looking to build practical skills, improve confidence, and access livelihood opportunities.

Who Can Apply

Rural youth seeking job-ready skills
Women looking to start or restart their careers
School and college students seeking practical learning
First-generation learners with limited exposure
Anyone with willingness to learn, practice, and grow

Fees & Support

Training is highly subsidized for learners. No rigid fee barrier for interested, motivated youth. Exact fee structure explained during counselling.

Work With Thalir Academy

We are building a community-driven learning ecosystem and welcome passionate individuals who want to contribute to meaningful grassroots impact.

Internship Opportunities

Field internships for social science, education, psychology, and development students. Gain hands-on experience in community outreach, training support, events, and impact documentation.
