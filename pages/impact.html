Impact Stories

From First Steps to Life Transformation

Behind every number is a personal journey. These stories reflect how skill development, counselling, and community support are transforming lives.

Rekeeshwari
Digital Skills → Panchayat Computer Assistant

Joined with very limited computer exposure. Through the Digital Skill programme she learned computer operations and digital tools step-by-step. After completing, she secured a position as Computer Assistant at a Panchayat Office — earning a stable income and performing her work independently.

Depunisha
BBA Student → Government Computer Assistant

Joined while pursuing her BBA degree, recognising the need for practical skills. Developed strong computer foundations and secured a Computer Assistant role within a government-linked system — stepping into the workforce before completing her studies.

Gokula Kannan
Computer & Tally Training → SBI Office

Was struggling to find stable employment when he joined Thalir Academy. Through Computer and Tally training, he developed technical competence and secured a position at a State Bank of India office — a significant step toward stable career and financial security.

Harishankari
From Social Restriction to Confidence

A young mother who faced strong social restrictions that limited her ability to work. Through training and continuous learning support, she transformed from a hesitant individual into someone confident and aware of her potential — aspiring toward financial independence for her children.

Nandini
Tailoring Program → Independent Livelihood

Joined the tailoring programme with no independent income, relying entirely on her family. Through training and Vastrika market linkages, she began receiving work and building her own income. Today she is financially independent and manages her household with confidence.

Prabhavati
30 Years Homebound → Self-Reliant

For over three decades, Prabhavati lived within her home with very limited outside exposure. Through training, counselling, and livelihood initiatives, she learned tailoring, generates income, and now encourages her children to learn. A deep transformation from restriction to independence.

Kavitha
16 Years at Home → Earning & Independent

Spent 16 years in household responsibilities with little outside exposure. After joining Thalir, she built and strengthened tailoring skills, found friendship and confidence in the supportive environment. Today she earns, supports her children, and makes independent decisions.

Manish
Class 8 Student → Aspiring Software Engineer

Joined the programme with only basic computer understanding. Through guided sessions with Paint, Notepad, and PowerPoint, what started as curiosity turned into confidence. Today he dreams of becoming a software engineer — a goal that seemed distant before digital access.

Updates

What's Happening at Thalir Academy

Parenting Awareness Programme

Focused awareness sessions for parents on emotional, physical, and social development of girl children — strengthening family support environments.

Learning Beyond Classrooms

Digital learning combined with weaving and craft sessions — developing both modern competencies and appreciation for local knowledge systems.

Creative & Holistic Development

Music and group activities providing spaces for self-expression, improving focus, emotional balance, and peer connection.

From Training to Employment

Multiple learners successfully transitioned into employment roles through structured interview prep, placement support, and post-placement follow-up.
