Who We Are

Community-Driven. Outcome-Focused.

Thalir Academy is a community-driven skill development initiative focused on empowering rural youth and women in Tamil Nadu, bridging the gap between education and employment.

We equip learners with practical skills, digital literacy, and real-world exposure, enabling them to secure jobs, start small businesses, and achieve financial independence.

Today, over 650+ youth and women have been empowered through our programs — transforming potential into sustainable livelihoods.

"We don't just teach skills — we build livelihoods"

✔100% practical, hands-on training
✔Focus on first-generation learners
✔Direct link to income opportunities
✔Strong community-based approach
✔Continuous mentorship and support
Our Purpose

Bridging the Gap Between Education and Livelihood

Thalir Academy was established in 2024 to address the growing gap between education, skills, and livelihood opportunities in rural Tamil Nadu. Many young people and women face limited access to skill training, digital education, and employment pathways. Thalir exists to change this by providing accessible, practical, and locally relevant training that leads to real income opportunities.

Our Journey

From Seed to 650+ Impacted Lives

2024

Foundation

Launched as Kalanjiyam Trust initiative with first computer & tailoring batches in Chengalpattu communities.

2025

Growth

100+ NSDC certifications (70% A/B grades), 50+ micro-enterprises, Ooraru Oli YouTube team formed with 500+ subscribers.

2026

Expansion

Third batch ongoing, beautician program launching, 200+ digital literacy target, advance tailoring expansion.
