Hire From Us

Access Job-Ready Talent from Rural Communities

Thalir learners are trained through structured, livelihood-focused programs combining technical learning, communication, discipline, and practical exposure. Our candidates are ready for entry-level roles with confidence and commitment.

  • → Data entry operators
  • → Computer assistants

  • → Aari embroidery specialists
  • → Beautician & grooming providers

🌟 Emerging Skills

  • → Communication & customer handling
  • → Community liaison roles

Why Hire From Thalir?

  • ✔ Pre-trained, job-ready candidates
  • ✔ Reduced hiring and training cost
  • ✔ High retention from local hiring
  • ✔ Social impact + business value

Partner With Us

Collaborate to Create Sustainable Impact

Partner with Thalir Academy to strengthen skill development, women's empowerment, employability, and livelihood opportunities. We welcome collaborations combining social commitment, local relevance, and measurable outcomes.

🏢 CSR Partnerships

Support skill-building, training infrastructure, certification, and community-based education through structured CSR engagement.

🏛 Institutional Tie-Ups

Schools, colleges, training institutions, and local bodies can expand access to practical skill development and youth engagement.

📚 Training Collaborations

Co-create industry-relevant training, short courses, workshops, guest lectures, and internship opportunities.

🛒 Market Linkages

Product exhibitions, bulk orders, service linkages, equipment support — connecting learners with local businesses.

What Partners Gain

✔Opportunity to support inclusive rural development
✔Access to a credible grassroots implementation partner
✔Visibility through meaningful social impact stories
✔Employee engagement, mentoring, and volunteering scope
✔Outcome-oriented collaboration with community reach
✔Stronger alignment between skilling and livelihood outcomes
