Our Purpose

Rooted in Community.
Built for Impact.

Thalir Academy, an initiative of Kalanjiyam Trust, addresses socio-economic challenges for rural youth and women with limited access to education and jobs. We blend vocational training, digital skills, entrepreneurship, and life skills.

🌍Uplift the socio-economic fabric of communities through sustainable skill training
📚Improve access to quality education and vocational certification pathways
💪Empower individuals with skills for personal and community growth
🚀Support entrepreneurship and livelihood creation through mentorship
